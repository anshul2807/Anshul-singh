MOTIVE

This website’s main motive is to help students as much as possible to make them learn the computer language they are interested in. It will provide various video lectures,problem statements,Quizzes and much more.

This website will also provide an online compiler with multiple languages which would help the students run their programs more fluently and help them save time and resource from downloading multiple compilers for multiple programming languages.

It will also act as an IDE for those students who don’t own a personal computer or a laptop or those who cannot choose what’s the right IDE for them.
